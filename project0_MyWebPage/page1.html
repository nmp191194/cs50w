We all wake up in the morning a little bit miserable, even just for a second. We wait for a sign, a ray of light maybe, to tell us the real world welcomes us back from our dreams. Funny how if we just look outside, we find that the world is already filled with light.

One afternoon you sit around, moping. The world outside shows little movements, unless you are really looking for them. It's all in your head now, calmness is what you let yourself feel. As time slowly ticks by, you drift away into an ocean of thoughts, of things past, of what is and is never to come.

Everyone deserves to be in love once, if not many times, and survive to declare it was real. Love that makes you brave, makes you mad, but after all, makes you who you are. It's a journey away from ourselves, into another soul, another universe, in hopes of finding ourselves back home.

I always prefer night-time. I think we see clearer in the dark. Seeing things that matter more clearly , that is. Night-time, for diurnal beings, is the time to stop fighting, hunting, hustling, to find shelter and be safe. It's only natural to feel more like myself at night, unaffected, undisturbed. Free.
